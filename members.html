Members

Our committed team at FC Berlin Nepal is what propels us toward achieving our common goal. We are a diverse and dedicated group of individuals joining together to pursue our shared objectives and ideals. Our team is made up of three distinct categories of members, each playing a unique and crucial role in our organization's journey.

General Members: The center and essence of our organization are our General Members. They contribute zeal, dedication, and a common love for our goal. These people are the foundation of our community; they give their time, ideas, and support to enable us to develop and accomplish our objectives. Our organization's general members are crucial to its development and to its friendly, inclusive culture.

General Playing Members: Our General Playing Members go above and beyond in their involvement with us because they are ardent supporters of our mission. They inspire us with their dedication and inspire others to get involved. These people actively participate in football and uphold the values of our organization in addition to being supporters of our cause.

Honorary Members: Our Honorary Members hold a special place within our organization. They are individuals who have significantly advanced our purpose and have played pivotal roles in the football and Nepalese culture. We honor these members for their exceptional commitment and assistance. Their knowledge and expertise continue to direct and motivate us as we work toward our objectives.

As a collective Team, we collaborate to promote a feeling of shared purpose, belonging, and teamwork. We embrace our differences and believe that our team diversity is what makes us more vibrant and stronger. We appreciate you joining our team and looking forward to the exciting journey ahead.
