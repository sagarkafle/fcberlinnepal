About Us

Welcome to FC Berlin Nepal Football Club

Are you ready for a football experience unlike any other? Here at FC Berlin Nepal, we combine the excitement of football with the essence of Nepalese culture right in the heart of Berlin. Our club is not just a team; it's a vibrant community where diverse cultures and the passion for football come together.

Our Story

FC Berlin Nepal was established in 2012 and has since grown to become a gathering place for both football and culture aficionados. Our adventure started with a group of friends who were passionate about football and had a strong desire to honor the diverse range of Nepalese customs in Germany's vibrant metropolis.

Our Mission

At FC Berlin Nepal, our mission is to foster unity and inclusivity through the universal language of football. We strive to create a sense of belonging where players from all backgrounds, and nationalities join together to share their love for football and honor Nepalese culture.

What we offer

Football Excellence: Our club boasts a passionate team of players and dedicated coaches who train rigorously to bring the best of football to the community. You'll feel at home here whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started.

Embracing Nepalese Culture: We at FC Berlin Nepal are proud to have successfully combined the fundamental elements of Nepalese culture with the global language of football. Everyone on our team has a unique experience as we celebrate unity and diversity. Our club is a true blend of cultures, from the vivid colors of our shirts to the traditional music that permeates the stadium during our games and celebrations thereafter.

Walk Together with Us

We extend a warm welcome to those who have an interest in football, regardless of experience level. Join the FC Berlin Nepal family to experience the special fusion of Nepalese culture and football. Our club offers a place for everyone, regardless of age, skill level, or background.

Stay Connected

Visit our Website to find out more about our club, the upcoming games, activities, and volunteer opportunities. Stay up to date with the latest news and experience FC Berlin Nepal's dynamic culture by following us on social media.
